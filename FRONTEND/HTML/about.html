Cougar Cafe

The University of Houston aims to prepare safe, nutritional food to the students at the Sugarland Campus. We cater to our staff and students in providing food for our community as well as uphold our sponsers who provide ingredients for our working kitchen. The University of Houston is partnered with the following organizations:

  • Food for Children Program
  • Healthy Foods Organization
  • Taco Bell Food Organization
  • Happy Food Great Food Organization
